Welcome to International Workshop 2022

The annual conference, Korea-Japan-China International Workshop will be held in Yeungnam University, Korea in October 27-28, 2022. This conference aims at improving the mutual understanding of educational and research activities of the three universities (Yeungnam University, Tohoku University, and Dalian University of Technology).

Since the first conference held at Yeungnam University in 2004, the three universities have hosted this annual ceremony every year, taking turns although since 2017, Dalian University of Technology has replaced UESTC of China.

Undergraduate and graduate students are encouraged to participate in this academic meeting as well as the faculty members.
It will be a great opportunity for sharing their university lives and recent research results in the area of electronics and communications.
